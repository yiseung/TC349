Groupwork for Google Chrome

Ever wanted work effectively with your group? Groupwork for Google Chrome is a Google Chrome Application that will make your group collaboration much easier and effective.

GroupWork will reside as a layer on top of Google Drive. The application will employ a “serverless” backend with most of the data residing either locally or in the user’s Google Drive storage, which will allow for powerful use of Google software. Using GroupWork will be quick and easy and will seem a natural outgrowth of user and web interaction. From project conception, to debate over project content, to implementation and finally to completion of a project, this application will guide you every step of the way.
